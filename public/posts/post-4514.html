The Importance of Peace Education in Catholic Schools

Catholic schools have a unique responsibility and opportunity to promote peace education as part of their mission to form students in the Gospel values. Peace education in Catholic schools goes beyond simply teaching about the absence of conflict to encompass the development of skills, attitudes, and values that promote harmony, justice, and reconciliation. Understanding the importance of peace education in Catholic schools helps you appreciate the role of education in building a more peaceful world and provides guidance for implementing peace education programs. Catholic schools demonstrate that education is a powerful tool for promoting peace and that young people can be effective peacemakers.

The Mission of Catholic Schools
