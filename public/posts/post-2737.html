What is Catholic Technology Ethics?

Catholic technology ethics is the application of Catholic moral principles and values to the use and development of technology, including artificial intelligence, social media, digital communication, and data privacy. This approach emphasizes human dignity, the common good, and responsible stewardship of technological advances while recognizing both the benefits and challenges of our digital age. Catholic teaching guides us to use technology as a tool for human flourishing rather than allowing it to dominate or dehumanize us.

The Foundation of Catholic Technology Ethics

Catholic technology ethics is built on several key principles:

  • Human dignity and the primacy of the person
  • Common good and social responsibility
  • Stewardship of creation and resources
  • Truth and authentic communication
  • Solidarity and human connection
