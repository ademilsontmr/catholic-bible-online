Joseph as Example for Husbands

2024-01-23

Introduction

Saint Joseph's relationship with Mary provides husbands with the perfect model of what it means to love and honor one's wife. In a world where marriage is often misunderstood and undervalued, Joseph shows us the true meaning of spousal love.

Respect and Reverence

Joseph's love for Mary was characterized by profound respect and reverence. When he learned of her miraculous pregnancy, his first thought was not for himself but for her protection and dignity. He planned to divorce her quietly to avoid exposing her to shame, demonstrating his concern for her reputation and well-being.

Pure and Selfless Love

This respect for Mary's dignity teaches husbands that true love always seeks the good of the beloved. It means putting your wife's needs, her dignity, and her spiritual welfare before your own desires. Joseph shows us that love is not about possession or control, but about service and sacrifice.

Chastity and Purity

Joseph's love was also marked by purity and chastity. His relationship with Mary was completely pure, free from selfish desires or lust. This purity allowed their love to be truly selfless and focused on God's plan for their family.

Spiritual Leadership

As a husband, Joseph was also a man of prayer and faith. He sought God's guidance in every decision, especially when it came to his relationship with Mary. This spiritual foundation is essential for any marriage.

Protective Love

Joseph's role as protector is another crucial aspect of his example for husbands. He protected Mary from physical danger, from social stigma, and from any threat to their family's well-being. Husbands are called to be the spiritual and physical protectors of their families.

Prayer

Saint Joseph, model for all husbands, teach us to love our wives with respect, purity, and selfless devotion. Help us to be spiritual leaders and protectors of our families. Amen.
