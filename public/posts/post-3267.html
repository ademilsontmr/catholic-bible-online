Jesus' Encounter as a Model of Evangelization

Jesus' encounter with the Samaritan woman at the well shows how to reach out to others with love and respect, breaking down barriers of prejudice and offering the gift of living water. Catholic tradition sees this encounter as essential for understanding that Jesus reaches out to all people, regardless of their background or past, and offers them the gift of eternal life. Jesus' encounter is not just about a conversation but about how to share the Gospel with others. This understanding shows that this encounter is not just about following traditions but about understanding evangelization. Jesus' encounter with the woman at the well helps believers understand how to share the Gospel.
