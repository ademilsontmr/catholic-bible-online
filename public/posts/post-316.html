The Love of Joseph for Mary and Jesus

2024-01-26

Introduction

The love that Saint Joseph had for Mary and Jesus was not an ordinary human love, but a love that was purified and elevated by grace. It was a love that reflected the very love of God Himself, making Joseph the perfect model for all family relationships.

Reverence and Respect for Mary

Joseph's love for Mary was characterized by profound reverence and respect. He loved her not for what she could give him, but for who she was - the chosen vessel of God, the Mother of the Savior. This love was completely selfless, seeking only her good and her happiness. When he learned of her miraculous pregnancy, his first concern was for her protection and dignity, not for his own feelings or reputation.

Complete Trust

This love was also marked by complete trust. Joseph trusted Mary completely, even when circumstances seemed to suggest otherwise. He trusted in her purity, her faithfulness, and her love for God. This trust was the foundation of their relationship, allowing them to face together the challenges and joys of their unique vocation.

Love for Jesus

Joseph's love for Jesus was equally profound and unique. He loved Jesus not as his biological son, but as the Son of God entrusted to his care. This love was marked by awe and wonder, as Joseph contemplated the mystery of the Incarnation in his daily life. Every day, he saw the face of God in the child Jesus, and this filled his heart with indescribable joy and love.

Practical and Protective Love

This love for Jesus was also practical and protective. Joseph worked tirelessly as a carpenter to provide for Jesus' material needs. He protected Jesus from physical danger, fleeing to Egypt when Herod threatened His life. He taught Jesus the skills of his trade, preparing Him for His future ministry.

Complete Self-Sacrifice

Joseph's love was also characterized by complete self-sacrifice. He put the needs of Mary and Jesus before his own comfort, his own desires, and even his own life. This self-sacrifice is the hallmark of true love - the willingness to give everything for the beloved, even one's own life.

Prayer

Saint Joseph, model of perfect love, help all families to love one another with the same purity, selflessness, and devotion that you showed to Mary and Jesus. May every family be a reflection of the love that existed in the Holy Family. Amen.
