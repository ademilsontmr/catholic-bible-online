The Solemnity of the Assumption of Mary

2025-02-02

Introduction: The Assumption

Defined by Pius XII in 1950, the dogma celebrates Mary’s full participation in her Son’s Resurrection.

Scripture and Tradition

While implicit in Scripture (Apoc 12; Lk 1:28), the faith of the Church consistently witnessed to Mary’s glorification.

Pastoral Meaning

Hope for the humble; dignity of the body; call to holiness.

Prayer

Mary assumed into heaven, draw our hearts to the things that are above. Amen.
