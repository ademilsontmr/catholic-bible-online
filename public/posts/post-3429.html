Indulgences as God's Mercy

Catholic indulgences are a beautiful expression of God's mercy, showing how He allows us to reduce or eliminate the temporal punishment due to sin through prayer and good works. Catholic tradition sees indulgences as essential for understanding that this practice is not just about reducing punishment but about God's mercy and love, and about His desire to help us grow in holiness. Indulgences are not just about punishment but about mercy and love. This understanding shows that indulgences are not just about following traditions but about understanding God's mercy. Indulgences help believers understand God's mercy.

Catholic tradition sees this foundation as essential for understanding the historical and theological significance of indulgences.
