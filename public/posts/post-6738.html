Why Politics Needs Saints and People of Faith

2024-12-19

Introduction: Politics Needs Saints

Politics desperately needs the witness and wisdom of saints and people of faith to become truly transformative and life-giving. Without the moral compass and spiritual vision that faith provides, politics can become a soulless exercise in power and self-interest that fails to serve the common good or respect human dignity.

Saints and people of faith bring to politics a perspective that transcends partisan interests and ideological divisions. They remind us that politics is ultimately about serving people and building a society where all can flourish as human persons created in the image of God.

What Saints Bring to Politics

Saints bring several important qualities to politics. First, they bring moral clarity and the courage to stand up for what is right, even when it's unpopular or politically costly. Second, they bring a vision of the common good that transcends narrow self-interest.

Third, they bring the wisdom to see political opponents not as enemies to be defeated, but as fellow human beings with whom they can work for the common good. Fourth, they bring the humility to recognize that they don't have all the answers and that they need to listen to others.

The Transformative Power of Faith

Faith has the power to transform politics from a power struggle into a ministry of love and service. When people of faith engage in politics, they bring Gospel values to bear on political questions and work for policies that respect human dignity and promote the common good.

This faith also gives them the strength to persevere in difficult political work and the hope to believe that positive change is possible even in challenging circumstances. Faith helps them see politics not as a necessary evil, but as a noble vocation of service to others.

Conclusion: A Call to Holiness

Politics needs saints and people of faith because it needs the moral vision and spiritual strength that only faith can provide. When people of faith engage in politics with holiness and wisdom, they become powerful instruments of God's love and justice in the world.

This engagement is not just about winning elections or advancing policies, but about transforming politics into a force for good that serves the common good and respects human dignity. When politics is guided by faith, it becomes truly transformative and life-giving.

Prayer for Faithful Politics

'Lord Jesus, help us to bring our faith to bear on politics and become saints in the political realm. Give us the wisdom to see politics as a vocation of service, and the courage to work for Your kingdom on earth. In Your name, Amen.'
