Introduction: The Foundation of Community

The role of friendship in building a Catholic community represents one of the most important aspects of Catholic ecclesiology and community life. This biblical foundation shows that the role of friendship in building a Catholic community is about recognizing that friendship is part of God's plan for building His Church. When we understand this, we can approach friendship as a communal responsibility.
