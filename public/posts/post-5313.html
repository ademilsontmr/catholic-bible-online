Marriage Rooted in God's Word (Matthew 7:24–25)

2025-01-27

The Biblical Foundation of Marriage Rooted in God's Word

Marriage rooted in God's Word in Catholic marriage is deeply rooted in Scripture, particularly in Matthew 7:24-25: "Everyone then who hears these words of mine and does them will be like a wise man who built his house on the rock. And the rain fell, and the floods came, and the winds blew and beat on that house, but it did not fall, because it had been founded on the rock." This passage shows that marriage rooted in God's Word is not just a nice concept but is a marriage built on a solid foundation that can withstand the storms of life. When couples understand marriage rooted in God's Word, they are not just being religious but are actively building their marriage on the solid foundation of God's Word.

This biblical foundation shows that marriage rooted in God's Word is not just about reading the Bible but is about actively living according to God's Word and building the marriage on this solid foundation. When couples understand this foundation, they are not just improving their relationship but are growing in holiness and becoming more faithful to their vocation. This understanding is not about being perfect but is about being willing to build on God's Word.

God's Word as the Solid Foundation

Marriage rooted in God's Word recognizes that God's Word is not just a book but is the solid foundation upon which strong marriages are built. Just as a house built on rock can withstand storms, so too can a marriage rooted in God's Word withstand the challenges and difficulties of life. This foundation is not about being rigid but is about being solid and reliable.

When couples understand marriage rooted in God's Word, they are not just being religious but are actively building their marriage on the solid foundation of God's Word. They are able to study God's Word together, to apply God's Word to their relationship, to seek God's guidance through His Word, to find strength and wisdom in God's Word, and to grow together in understanding and applying God's Word. This foundation becomes a powerful source of strength and stability.

The Daily Practice of Being Rooted in God's Word

Understanding marriage rooted in God's Word is practiced in the small, everyday moments of married life. This includes studying God's Word together, applying God's Word to the relationship, seeking God's guidance through His Word, finding strength and wisdom in God's Word, and growing together in understanding and applying God's Word. These daily practices of being rooted in God's Word build a strong foundation for the relationship.

This daily practice includes being willing to study God's Word together regularly and to discuss its meaning, being willing to apply God's Word to one's relationship and to live according to its teachings, being willing to seek God's guidance through His Word when making decisions, being willing to find strength and wisdom in God's Word when facing challenges, and being willing to grow together in understanding and applying God's Word to one's marriage. When couples practice being rooted in God's Word in these small ways, they are building a marriage that is strong and stable.

Being Rooted in God's Word and Communication

Understanding marriage rooted in God's Word is closely connected to communication. When couples are able to communicate about God's Word and its application to their marriage, they are better able to be rooted in God's Word effectively. Good communication helps couples to study God's Word together, to discuss its meaning, and to apply it to their relationship.

This communication for being rooted in God's Word includes being willing to study God's Word together and to discuss its meaning, being willing to apply God's Word to one's relationship and to discuss how to live according to its teachings, being willing to seek God's guidance through His Word together, being willing to find strength and wisdom in God's Word together, and being willing to grow together in understanding and applying God's Word. When couples communicate in these ways, they are not just sharing information but are building a relationship based on God's Word.

Being Rooted in God's Word and Growth

Understanding marriage rooted in God's Word is essential for personal and spiritual growth. When couples are rooted in God's Word, they are growing in virtues like wisdom, understanding, and faithfulness. These virtues help them to become better people and better spouses. They also help them to become better witnesses to God's love in the world.

This growth through being rooted in God's Word includes growing in wisdom as they learn to understand and apply God's Word, growing in understanding as they learn to comprehend God's teachings, growing in faithfulness as they learn to be faithful to God's Word, growing in strength as they learn to find strength in God's Word, and growing in holiness as they learn to live according to God's Word. When couples grow in these virtues, they are not just improving their relationship but are growing in holiness and becoming more like Christ.

Conclusion: A Rooted Love

Marriage rooted in God's Word in Catholic marriage is a rooted love that helps couples to grow in holiness and to build a strong and lasting relationship. When couples understand marriage rooted in God's Word, they are not just being religious but are actively building their marriage on the solid foundation of God's Word.

As we reflect on marriage rooted in God's Word, let us remember that this foundation is not just a human concept but is a divine foundation that requires God's grace. When we are rooted in God's Word, we are building a marriage that is based on wisdom, strength, and the grace of God.
