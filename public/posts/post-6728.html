How Politics Can Become a Path to Holiness

2024-12-19

Introduction: Politics as Spiritual Path

Politics can become a path to holiness when it's approached with the right intentions, virtues, and spiritual perspective. Rather than being seen as a necessary evil or a worldly distraction, political engagement can be transformed into a spiritual discipline that helps us grow in virtue and draw closer to God.

This understanding of politics as a path to holiness is rooted in the Catholic teaching that all legitimate human activities can be sanctified and offered to God. When we engage in politics out of love for God and neighbor, with the intention of serving the common good, our political activity becomes a form of worship and a means of growing in holiness.

The Virtues Required for Holy Politics

To make politics a path to holiness, certain virtues are essential. Humility helps us recognize that we don't have all the answers and that we need to listen to others, especially those who disagree with us. Charity motivates us to work for the good of others rather than our own advancement.

Prudence helps us make wise decisions that consider both immediate needs and long-term consequences. Justice ensures that we work for the good of all, not just some. Fortitude gives us the courage to stand up for what is right, even when it's unpopular or politically costly.

Transforming Political Activity into Prayer

Politics becomes a path to holiness when we transform our political activity into prayer. This means offering our political work to God, asking for His guidance in our decisions, and recognizing that we are cooperating with Him in building His kingdom on earth.

It means seeing political opponents not as enemies to be defeated, but as fellow human beings created in the image of God, with whom we can work for the common good. It means being willing to make sacrifices and compromises for the sake of others, following the example of Christ who gave His life for us.

Conclusion: A Sacred Vocation

Politics can indeed become a path to holiness when it's approached with the right intentions and virtues. This understanding transforms political engagement from a worldly activity into a sacred vocation that helps us grow in holiness and serve God's kingdom.

When we engage in politics as a path to holiness, we become instruments of God's love and justice in the world. We help build a society that reflects the dignity and worth of every human person, a society that is more just, more compassionate, and more human.

Prayer for Holy Politics

'Lord Jesus, help us to make our political engagement a path to holiness. Give us the virtues we need to serve You in the political realm, and may our political work become a form of worship and prayer. In Your name, Amen.'
