Holiness Through Striving for Sainthood in Everyday Life

2024-12-20

Introduction: Sainthood as Universal Call

Holiness through striving for sainthood in everyday life is about recognizing that sainthood is not just for a few special people or for those who live extraordinary lives, but the universal call that God gives to every Christian and the path to true happiness and fulfillment. When we strive for sainthood in everyday life, we're not just being pious or trying to be perfect, but responding to God's call to become the people He created us to be and to find our true purpose and meaning in life. This striving is not about achieving some impossible standard of perfection, but about allowing God to work in us and to transform us more and more into the image of His Son.

Many people think that sainthood is impossible or that it's only for very holy people who live in monasteries or who have special spiritual gifts. However, the Catholic understanding is that sainthood is possible for everyone, and that it's achieved not through extraordinary feats or dramatic experiences, but through the ordinary activities and challenges of daily life. When we strive for sainthood in everyday life, we're not just trying to be good people, but allowing God to work in us and through us to bring His love and truth to the world.

How to Strive for Sainthood in Daily Life

Striving for sainthood in everyday life involves several important practices. First, it means recognizing that every moment and every activity is an opportunity for holiness, whether we're working, praying, eating, or simply being present to others. Second, it means being willing to make small sacrifices and to practice virtue in ordinary situations, recognizing that holiness is built through daily choices and actions. Third, it means being aware of God's presence throughout the day and being willing to turn to Him in prayer and thanksgiving. Fourth, it means being willing to accept difficulties and challenges as opportunities for growth and purification. Fifth, it means being grateful for the gift of life and for all the ways God blesses us each day.

Conclusion: Sainthood as Fulfillment

Striving for sainthood in everyday life leads to holiness because it helps us to recognize that every moment is an opportunity for growth and transformation. When we strive for sainthood, we're not just trying to be good people, but allowing God to work in us and to make us into the people He created us to be. This striving helps us to become more aware of God's presence, more grateful for His gifts, and more willing to allow Him to work in us and through us. We become saints not by doing extraordinary things, but by allowing God to do extraordinary things through our ordinary lives.

Prayer for Sainthood

'Lord Jesus, help us to strive for sainthood in everyday life, recognizing that this is Your call to us and the path to true happiness and fulfillment. Give us the grace to see every moment as an opportunity for holiness, and help us to be willing to make small sacrifices and to practice virtue in ordinary situations. May our striving for sainthood lead us to greater holiness and help us to become the people You created us to be. In Your name, Amen.'
