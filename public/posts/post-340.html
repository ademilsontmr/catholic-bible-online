The Nativity of the Blessed Virgin Mary

2025-02-02

Introduction: The Nativity of Mary

The Church celebrates the birth of Mary as the dawn that precedes the Sun of Justice. In her, God prepares a humble dwelling for His Son.

Meaning of the Feast

  • Grace in history: Mary’s birth announces salvation near at hand.
  • Joy and simplicity: God chooses the lowly to accomplish great things.
  • Family of faith: Mary’s life nurtures our filial trust in God.

Scripture Echoes

While Scripture is silent on her birth, Luke 1–2 presents Mary as the faithful Daughter of Zion. Tradition preserves names of her parents, Saints Joachim and Anne, as models of hope.

How to Celebrate

Pray the Rosary; thank God for your baptismal calling; practice a small work of mercy in Mary’s honor.

Prayer

Heavenly Father, in Mary’s birth You gave the world hope. Form our hearts after hers, that we may welcome Your Son more perfectly. Amen.
