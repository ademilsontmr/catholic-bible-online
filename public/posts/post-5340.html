The Power of Blessing in Marriage (Numbers 6:24–26)

2025-01-27

The Biblical Foundation of Blessing in Marriage

The power of blessing in marriage in Catholic marriage is deeply rooted in Scripture, particularly in Numbers 6:24-26: "The Lord bless you and keep you; the Lord make his face to shine upon you and be gracious to you; the Lord lift up his countenance upon you and give you peace." This passage shows that the power of blessing in marriage is not just a nice concept but is a divine reality where God's blessing brings protection, grace, and peace. When couples understand the power of blessing in marriage, they are not just being religious but are actively seeking and receiving God's blessing and becoming channels of His blessing to each other.

This biblical foundation shows that the power of blessing in marriage is not just about giving compliments but is about actively seeking and receiving God's blessing and becoming channels of His blessing to each other. When couples understand this power, they are not just improving their relationship but are growing in holiness and becoming more faithful to their vocation. This understanding is not about being perfect but is about being willing to seek and receive God's blessing.

Blessing as Divine Power

The power of blessing in marriage recognizes that blessing is not just a nice word but is a divine power that brings protection, grace, and peace. Just as God's blessing brings protection, grace, and peace, so too does blessing in marriage bring these same divine gifts to the relationship. This blessing is not about being magical but is about being powerful and effective.

When couples understand the power of blessing in marriage, they are not just being religious but are actively seeking and receiving God's blessing. They are able to seek God's blessing for their marriage, to become channels of God's blessing to each other, to receive God's protection, grace, and peace, to bless each other with God's blessing, and to be examples of God's blessing to others. This blessing becomes a powerful source of divine grace and protection.

The Daily Practice of Seeking and Giving Blessing

Understanding the power of blessing in marriage is practiced in the small, everyday moments of married life. This includes seeking God's blessing for the marriage, becoming channels of God's blessing to each other, receiving God's protection, grace, and peace, blessing each other with God's blessing, and being examples of God's blessing to others. These daily practices of seeking and giving blessing build a strong foundation for the relationship.

This daily practice includes being willing to seek God's blessing for one's marriage and to pray for His blessing regularly, being willing to become a channel of God's blessing to one's spouse and to bless them with God's blessing, being willing to receive God's protection, grace, and peace and to be open to these gifts, being willing to bless one's spouse with God's blessing and to speak words of blessing, and being willing to be an example of God's blessing to others and to share this blessing. When couples practice seeking and giving blessing in these small ways, they are building a marriage that is strong and blessed.

Seeking and Giving Blessing and Communication

Understanding the power of blessing in marriage is closely connected to communication. When couples are able to communicate about their desire for God's blessing and their commitment to being channels of blessing, they are better able to seek and give blessing effectively. Good communication helps couples to pray together for blessing, to share their desire for blessing, and to work together in being channels of blessing.

This communication for seeking and giving blessing includes being willing to pray together for God's blessing on the marriage, being willing to discuss one's desire for God's blessing and to share this desire with one's spouse, being willing to bless each other with words of blessing and to speak God's blessing over each other, being willing to discuss how to be channels of God's blessing to others, and being willing to encourage each other in seeking and giving blessing. When couples communicate in these ways, they are not just sharing information but are building a relationship based on blessing and grace.

Seeking and Giving Blessing and Growth

Understanding the power of blessing in marriage is essential for personal and spiritual growth. When couples seek and give blessing, they are growing in virtues like grace, peace, and blessing. These virtues help them to become better people and better spouses. They also help them to become better witnesses to God's love in the world.

This growth through seeking and giving blessing includes growing in grace as they learn to receive and give God's grace, growing in peace as they learn to receive and share God's peace, growing in blessing as they learn to be channels of God's blessing, growing in prayer as they learn to pray for God's blessing, and growing in witness as they learn to be examples of God's blessing to others. When couples grow in these virtues, they are not just improving their relationship but are growing in holiness and becoming more like Christ.

Conclusion: A Blessed Marriage

The power of blessing in marriage in Catholic marriage is a blessed marriage that helps couples to grow in holiness and to build a strong and lasting relationship. When couples understand the power of blessing in marriage, they are not just being religious but are actively seeking and receiving God's blessing and becoming channels of His blessing to each other.

As we reflect on the power of blessing in marriage, let us remember that this blessing is not just a human word but is a divine power that requires God's grace. When we seek and give blessing, we are building a marriage that is based on grace, peace, and the blessing of God.
