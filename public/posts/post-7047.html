Introduction: Immigrants as Faith Builders

The role of immigrant Catholics in building America's faith is about recognizing that immigrant Catholics are not just newcomers or temporary residents, but vital contributors to the growth and vitality of the Catholic Church in the United States, who bring with them rich traditions, deep faith, and a commitment to building up the Church and serving God and others.
