Understanding the Sacraments

The sacraments are sacred signs instituted by Christ that give grace. They include baptism, confirmation, Eucharist, reconciliation, anointing of the sick, holy orders, and matrimony. The sacraments are not just religious ceremonies but are powerful means of God's grace working in our lives. Understanding the sacraments helps you recognize what they are and why they are important. Understanding the sacraments helps you recognize what this involves and why it is important.
