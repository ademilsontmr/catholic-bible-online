Holiness Through Perseverance in Marriage Vows

2024-12-20

Introduction: Marriage as a Vocation to Holiness

Holiness through perseverance in marriage vows is about recognizing that marriage is not just a social contract or a romantic relationship, but a sacred vocation that God has given us for our sanctification and the salvation of our souls. When we make our marriage vows, we're not just making promises to our spouse, but to God Himself, and these vows are meant to be kept for life. Perseverance in marriage vows is not about staying married because it's convenient or because we have no other options, but about remaining faithful to God's call and growing in holiness through this commitment.

Marriage is often romanticized in our culture, with the focus on the wedding day and the early years of marriage. However, the reality of marriage is that it involves daily choices to love, to forgive, to serve, and to remain faithful even when it's difficult. These choices are not always easy, and they often require sacrifice and self-denial. However, it's precisely through these difficult choices that we grow in holiness and become more like Christ, who loved us to the point of giving His life for us.

The Challenges of Marriage

Perseverance in marriage vows involves facing and overcoming many challenges. These challenges can include financial difficulties, health problems, communication issues, conflicts over parenting, and the natural changes that occur as both spouses grow and change over time. These challenges are not signs that the marriage is failing, but opportunities for growth in virtue and for deepening our love for one another. When we face these challenges together with faith, hope, and love, we grow not just as individuals, but as a couple united in Christ.

Conclusion: Marriage as Sanctification

Perseverance in marriage vows leads to holiness because it helps us to practice the virtues that are essential for spiritual growth: patience, forgiveness, self-sacrifice, and unconditional love. When we remain faithful to our marriage vows, we're not just staying married, but allowing God to transform us through this commitment. We become more patient, more forgiving, more selfless, and more loving. Our marriage becomes a witness to Christ's love for the Church and a means of our own sanctification.

Prayer for Marriage Vows

'Lord Jesus, help us to persevere in our marriage vows and to remain faithful to the commitment we made to You and to our spouse. Give us the grace to love one another as You love us, to forgive quickly, and to serve one another with humility and love. May our marriage be a witness to Your love and help us grow in holiness. In Your name, Amen.'
