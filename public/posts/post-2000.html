What is Environmental Stewardship?

Environmental stewardship is the practice of caring for God's creation with love, responsibility, and wisdom, recognizing that the earth and all its resources are gifts from God that we are called to protect and preserve for future generations. The Catholic Church teaches that environmental stewardship is not just an environmental issue, but a moral and spiritual responsibility that flows from our faith in God as Creator. This practice involves making choices that respect and protect the environment, reducing our impact on the earth, and working to ensure that all people have access to clean air, water, and natural resources. It is about recognizing that we are part of God's creation and that we have a responsibility to care for it, not just for our own benefit, but for the good of all people and for the glory of God. Environmental stewardship is also about recognizing the interconnectedness of all creation and understanding that our actions affect not just the environment, but also the poor and vulnerable who often suffer most from environmental degradation.

The Bible provides many teachings about environmental stewardship, from the creation story in Genesis where God gives humans dominion over the earth, to the many references to God's care for creation, to the call to be good stewards of all that God has given us. Scripture shows us that God loves His creation and that He has entrusted it to our care. The Bible also teaches that we will be held accountable for how we use and care for the gifts that God has given us, and that caring for creation is part of our responsibility as God's children.

How to Pray for Environmental Stewardship

When praying for environmental stewardship, it's important to approach God with gratitude for His creation and a sincere desire to care for it responsibly. Begin by thanking God for the gift of creation and asking Him to help you be a good steward of all that He has given you. You might start your prayer by saying something like, 'Heavenly Father, thank You for the gift of Your beautiful creation and for entrusting it to our care. Please help me to be a good steward of the earth and to make choices that honor You and care for Your creation.' Take time to reflect on how your daily choices affect the environment and ask God to help you make better decisions.

Ask God to help you understand the impact of your choices and to give you the wisdom to make good decisions about how you use resources. You might say something like, 'Lord, help me to understand how my choices affect the environment and to make decisions that care for Your creation. Give me the wisdom to know how to reduce my impact on the earth and to work for justice for those who are most affected by environmental problems.' Pray for the courage to make changes in your life and to advocate for environmental justice, even when it's difficult or unpopular.

What to Pray For

When praying for environmental stewardship, focus on several key areas. Pray for wisdom in making environmental choices, asking God to help you understand the impact of your decisions and to make choices that care for creation. Pray for the courage to make changes in your life, asking God to help you reduce your environmental impact and to live more sustainably. Pray for justice for those affected by environmental problems, asking God to help you work for the good of all people, especially the poor and vulnerable who often suffer most from environmental degradation.

Frequency of Prayer

Prayer for environmental stewardship should be regular and consistent, ideally daily, as caring for creation requires ongoing attention and commitment.
