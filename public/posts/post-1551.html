Introduction: Faith and Social Ministry

The relationship between faith and Catholic social ministry represents a fundamental aspect of Catholic social teaching that recognizes how faith provides the foundation and motivation for serving others and working for justice and peace in society. The Catholic understanding of the relationship between faith and social ministry emphasizes that faith provides the foundation for understanding that we are called to serve others as Christ served us, helps us recognize the dignity of every person and our responsibility to care for them, and motivates us to work for justice and peace in society.
