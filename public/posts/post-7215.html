Introduction: Contributions as Life Foundation

Faith, hope, and service through Catholic contributions to American life is about recognizing that the Catholic faith is not just a personal belief system or a collection of religious practices, but a comprehensive approach to contributing to American life that integrates faith, hope, and service in ways that help American citizens to live meaningful and fulfilling lives while working for the common good. Throughout American history, Catholic contributions to American life have played a vital role in American society, helping to guide various forms of personal development and community building that have contributed to the moral and spiritual character of the nation. When American Catholics make these contributions effectively, they can help to transform American society and to create a more just and compassionate nation.
