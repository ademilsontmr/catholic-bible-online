Introduction: Saints as Models Foundation

This public life inspiration is not about seeking personal spiritual experiences or about avoiding the challenges of public service, but about finding the spiritual strength and guidance needed to serve in public life with virtue and to contribute to the common good more effectively.

When American Catholics look to these saints for inspiration effectively in public life, they can help to transform the Catholic Church in America and to create a more vibrant and committed community of faith.
