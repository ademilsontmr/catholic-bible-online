Novena to St. Anne for Married Couples: A Catholic Prayer Guide

2025-02-02

Who is St. Anne?

Saint Anne, the mother of the Blessed Virgin Mary and grandmother of Jesus, is a powerful intercessor for married couples and families. As the mother of Mary, she played a crucial role in raising the woman who would become the Mother of God, and her example of faith, love, and devotion continues to inspire couples today.

Saint Anne is particularly known for her patience and trust in God during the many years she and her husband Joachim were childless. Her prayers were eventually answered when she conceived Mary, who would become the greatest of all women. This experience makes her especially sympathetic to couples who are struggling with fertility issues or other challenges in their marriage.

Saint Anne's feast day is celebrated on July 26th, and she is the patron saint of married couples, mothers, grandmothers, and those who are childless.

Why Pray to St. Anne for Married Couples?

Saint Anne is an excellent intercessor for married couples for several reasons:

Her Experience with Marriage

Saint Anne was married to Saint Joachim and experienced the joys and challenges of married life. She understands the struggles that couples face and can intercede for them with great compassion.

Her Patience and Trust

Saint Anne's patience during her years of childlessness and her trust in God's plan make her a powerful example for couples who are facing difficulties in their marriage.

Her Role in Salvation History

As the mother of Mary, Saint Anne played a crucial role in salvation history. Her prayers and intercession can help couples to understand their own role in God's plan.

Her Love for Family

Saint Anne's love for her daughter Mary and her grandson Jesus shows her deep understanding of family love and relationships.

The Novena Structure

The novena to Saint Anne for married couples is prayed for nine consecutive days. Each day includes:

Opening Prayer (Pray each day)

Glorious Saint Anne, you are filled with compassion for those who invoke you and with love for those who suffer. Heavily burdened with the weight of my troubles, I cast myself at your feet and humbly beg of you to take the present intention which I recommend to you in your special care.

Please recommend it to your daughter, the Blessed Virgin Mary, and place it before the throne of Jesus, so that He may bring it to a happy issue. Continue to intercede for me until my request is granted. But, above all, obtain for me the grace one day to see my God face to face, and with you and Mary and all the angels and saints to praise and bless Him for all eternity. Amen.

Daily Prayer (Pray the appropriate day's prayer)

Day 1: Saint Anne, you who were blessed with the gift of a holy marriage to Saint Joachim, help us to strengthen our marriage and to grow in love for each other. Teach us to be patient, kind, and forgiving toward each other, and to always seek God's will in our relationship.

Day 2: Saint Anne, you who trusted in God during your years of childlessness, help us to trust in God's plan for our marriage and family. Give us the patience to wait for God's timing and the faith to believe that He has a perfect plan for us.

Day 3: Saint Anne, you who raised the Blessed Virgin Mary, help us to be good parents and to raise our children in the faith. Guide us in teaching our children about God and in helping them to grow in holiness.

Day 4: Saint Anne, you who were a model of faith and devotion, help us to grow in our faith and to make God the center of our marriage. Teach us to pray together and to support each other in our spiritual journey.

Day 5: Saint Anne, you who experienced the joys and challenges of family life, help us to find joy in our marriage and to face challenges with courage and faith. Give us the strength to overcome difficulties together.

Day 6: Saint Anne, you who were a loving grandmother to Jesus, help us to be loving and supportive of our extended family. Teach us to show love and respect to our in-laws and to build strong family relationships.

Day 7: Saint Anne, you who were patient and trusting in God's timing, help us to be patient with each other and with God's plan for our marriage. Give us the wisdom to know when to act and when to wait.

Day 8: Saint Anne, you who were a woman of prayer, help us to make prayer a regular part of our marriage. Teach us to pray together, to pray for each other, and to seek God's guidance in all our decisions.

Day 9: Saint Anne, you who are now in heaven with your daughter and grandson, help us to keep our eyes fixed on heaven and to remember that our marriage is a path to holiness. Pray for us that we may one day join you in praising God for all eternity.

Closing Prayer (Pray each day)

O glorious Saint Anne, you are filled with compassion for those who invoke you and with love for those who suffer. Heavily burdened with the weight of my troubles, I cast myself at your feet and humbly beg of you to take the present intention which I recommend to you in your special care.

Please recommend it to your daughter, the Blessed Virgin Mary, and place it before the throne of Jesus, so that He may bring it to a happy issue. Continue to intercede for me until my request is granted. But, above all, obtain for me the grace one day to see my God face to face, and with you and Mary and all the angels and saints to praise and bless Him for all eternity. Amen.

Additional Prayers for Married Couples

Here are some additional prayers you can pray to Saint Anne:

Prayer for Marriage Strengthening

Saint Anne, you who were blessed with a holy marriage, help us to strengthen our marriage and to grow in love for each other. Teach us to be patient, kind, and forgiving, and to always seek God's will in our relationship. Help us to communicate with love and respect, and to support each other in our journey toward holiness. Amen.

Prayer for Fertility

Saint Anne, you who experienced the blessing of motherhood after years of waiting, help us to trust in God's plan for our family. If it is God's will, bless us with children and help us to raise them in the faith. If we are called to a different path, help us to find joy and fulfillment in God's plan for us. Amen.

Prayer for Family Unity

Saint Anne, you who were a loving grandmother and mother, help us to build strong family relationships. Teach us to love and support our extended family, to be patient with each other's weaknesses, and to always seek unity and peace in our family. Amen.

Conclusion

The novena to Saint Anne for married couples is a powerful way to seek her intercession for your marriage. Saint Anne understands the challenges and joys of married life, and she is always ready to help couples who turn to her with trust and devotion.

Remember that Saint Anne is not just an intercessor, but also a model of faith, patience, and love. By praying this novena, you are not only asking for her help, but also learning from her example of how to live a holy marriage.

May Saint Anne intercede for your marriage and help you to grow in love, faith, and holiness together. Amen.
