Encouraging Each Other Daily (Hebrews 3:13)

2025-01-27

The Biblical Foundation of Daily Encouragement

Encouraging each other daily in Catholic marriage is deeply rooted in Scripture, particularly in Hebrews 3:13: "But exhort one another every day, as long as it is called 'today,' that none of you may be hardened by the deceitfulness of sin." This passage shows that daily encouragement is not just a nice thing to do but is essential for preventing the hardening of hearts and staying faithful to God. When couples encourage each other daily, they are not just being supportive but are actively participating in God's work of keeping each other's hearts soft and faithful.

This biblical foundation shows that daily encouragement is not just about giving compliments but is about actively exhorting each other to stay faithful to God and to avoid the deceitfulness of sin. When couples encourage each other daily, they are not just improving their relationship but are growing in holiness and becoming more faithful to their vocation. This encouragement is not about being perfect but is about being willing to support each other in their faith journey.

Daily Encouragement as Protection

Encouraging each other daily in Catholic marriage serves as protection against the hardening of hearts and the deceitfulness of sin. Just as the early Christians were called to exhort each other daily to prevent hardening of hearts, so too are married couples called to encourage each other daily to stay faithful to God and to each other. This encouragement is not about being critical but is about being supportive and encouraging.

When couples encourage each other daily, they are not just being supportive but are actively protecting each other from the hardening of hearts and the deceitfulness of sin. They are able to support each other in their faith journey, to encourage each other to stay faithful to God, to help each other avoid sin and temptation, to build each other up in love and faith, and to keep each other's hearts soft and open to God's grace. This encouragement becomes a powerful protection for their marriage.

The Daily Practice of Encouragement

Practicing daily encouragement is practiced in the small, everyday moments of married life. This includes supporting each other in their faith journey, encouraging each other to stay faithful to God, helping each other avoid sin and temptation, building each other up in love and faith, and keeping each other's hearts soft and open to God's grace. These daily practices of encouragement build a strong foundation for the relationship.

This daily practice includes being willing to support one's spouse in their faith journey and to encourage them to stay close to God, being willing to encourage one's spouse to stay faithful to God and to avoid sin and temptation, being willing to help one's spouse recognize and avoid the deceitfulness of sin, being willing to build one's spouse up in love and faith through words and actions, and being willing to help keep one's spouse's heart soft and open to God's grace. When couples practice daily encouragement in these small ways, they are building a marriage that is strong and faithful.

Daily Encouragement and Communication

Practicing daily encouragement is closely connected to communication. When couples are able to communicate encouragement effectively, they are better able to support each other in their faith journey. Good communication helps couples to express their support, to share their encouragement, and to build each other up in love and faith.

This communication for daily encouragement includes being willing to express one's support and encouragement to one's spouse regularly, being willing to share words of encouragement and affirmation, being willing to discuss one's faith journey and how one can support each other, being willing to encourage one's spouse to stay faithful to God, and being willing to build each other up in love and faith through communication. When couples communicate in these ways, they are not just sharing information but are building a relationship based on encouragement and support.

Daily Encouragement and Growth

Practicing daily encouragement is essential for personal and spiritual growth. When couples encourage each other daily, they are growing in virtues like love, faithfulness, and support. These virtues help them to become better people and better spouses. They also help them to become better witnesses to God's love in the world.

This growth through daily encouragement includes growing in love as they learn to love each other more supportively, growing in faithfulness as they learn to help each other stay faithful to God, growing in support as they learn to support each other in their faith journey, growing in wisdom as they learn to recognize and avoid sin, and growing in holiness as they learn to keep each other's hearts soft and open to God's grace. When couples grow in these virtues, they are not just improving their relationship but are growing in holiness and becoming more like Christ.

Conclusion: An Encouraging Love

Encouraging each other daily in Catholic marriage is an encouraging love that helps couples to grow in holiness and to build a strong and lasting relationship. When couples encourage each other daily, they are not just being supportive but are actively participating in God's work of keeping each other's hearts soft and faithful.

As we reflect on encouraging each other daily, let us remember that this encouragement is not just a human effort but is a divine protection that requires God's grace. When we encourage each other daily, we are building a marriage that is based on support, faithfulness, and the grace of God.
