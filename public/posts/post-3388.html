The Communion of Saints

All Saints' Day celebrates the communion of saints, showing how all believers, both in heaven and on earth, are united in Christ and can pray for one another. All Saints' Day is connected to All Souls' Day, showing how we celebrate the saints in heaven on November 1st and pray for the souls in purgatory on November 2nd. Catholic tradition sees this connection as essential for understanding that these two days are connected and that they help us to understand the different states of the afterlife. The connection shows us that these days are connected. This connection shows that All Saints' Day is not just about prayer but about connection.
