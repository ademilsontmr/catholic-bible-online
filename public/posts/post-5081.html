Teaching Kids About the Power of Intercessory Prayer

Teaching kids about the power of intercessory prayer is not just about telling them to pray for others but about helping them understand that praying for others is a powerful way to show love, participate in God's work, and make a real difference in the lives of those they pray for. The Biblical Foundation: Pray for One Another

The Bible teaches us about the importance of intercessory prayer. James 5:16 instructs us to 'confess your sins to each other and pray for each other so that you may be healed. The prayer of a righteous person is powerful and effective.' Additionally, 1 Timothy 2:1 tells us to 'pray for all people.' Understanding this biblical foundation helps you recognize that intercessory prayer is important to God.
