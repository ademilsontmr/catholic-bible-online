Catholic Reflections on Peace During the Jubilee Year

The Jubilee Year, a special time of grace and mercy proclaimed by the Church, provides unique opportunities for reflection and action on peace. This holy year emphasizes forgiveness, reconciliation, and renewal, which are essential elements of peace building. Understanding Catholic reflections on peace during the Jubilee Year helps you appreciate the Church's emphasis on peace through mercy and provides guidance for participating in this important time. The Jubilee Year demonstrates that peace comes through God's mercy and our response to that mercy.
