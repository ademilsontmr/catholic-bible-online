Introduction: Spiritual Care in Healthcare

The role of Catholic chaplains in hospital settings represents a vital ministry that provides spiritual care and support to patients, families, and healthcare staff during some of the most challenging moments of life. Catholic chaplains, who are ordained priests or trained lay ministers, serve as spiritual caregivers who bring the presence of God and the comfort of the Church to those who are suffering, afraid, or in need of spiritual support.
