Saint Michael's Help in Parenting

Saint Michael can also help parents in their role of raising children. He can provide parents with wisdom and guidance in making decisions about their children's welfare, and he can help parents to create a safe and holy environment for their children to grow and develop.

Conclusion: A Faithful Protector

Saint Michael's role in protecting children demonstrates his special care for the innocent and vulnerable. This role gives parents confidence that their children are protected and that they have a powerful heavenly ally to help them in their sacred duty of raising their children.
