The Infant Jesus as a Model of Humility and Trust

Devotion to the Infant Jesus focuses on Jesus as a helpless baby, teaching us about humility, trust, and the mystery of the Incarnation. Catholic tradition sees the Infant Jesus as essential for understanding that God became a helpless baby to show us the way of humility and to invite us to trust in Him. The Infant Jesus is not just about a cute image but about the profound mystery of God's humility and love. This understanding shows that devotion to the Infant Jesus is not just about following traditions but about understanding the mystery of the Incarnation. The Infant Jesus helps believers understand God's humility and love.
