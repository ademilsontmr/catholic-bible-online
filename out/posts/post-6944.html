Holiness Through Listening to the Voice of Conscience

2024-12-20

Introduction: Conscience as God's Voice

Holiness through listening to the voice of conscience is about recognizing that conscience is not just a feeling or a personal opinion, but God's voice speaking to us in the depths of our hearts, guiding us toward what is good and away from what is evil. Conscience is not something we create or choose, but a gift from God that helps us to know right from wrong and to make good moral choices. When we listen to our conscience, we're not just following our feelings, but listening to God Himself and allowing Him to guide us on the path to holiness.

Many people today are confused about what conscience is and how to use it properly. Some think that conscience is just a feeling that can be ignored or overridden when it's inconvenient. Others think that conscience is completely subjective and that everyone can decide for themselves what is right and wrong. However, the Catholic understanding of conscience is that it's God's voice within us, but that it needs to be properly formed and educated to speak clearly and accurately. When we listen to a well-formed conscience, we're listening to God's guidance for our lives.

Forming and Following Conscience

Listening to the voice of conscience involves several important practices. First, it means forming our conscience through prayer, study of Scripture and Church teaching, and spiritual direction. Second, it means being willing to listen to our conscience even when it's difficult or when it goes against our desires or the opinions of others. Third, it means acting on what our conscience tells us, even when it requires sacrifice or courage. Fourth, it means being willing to admit when we've made mistakes and to seek forgiveness and reconciliation.

Conclusion: Conscience as Guide

Listening to the voice of conscience leads to holiness because it helps us to make good moral choices and to grow in virtue. When we follow our conscience, we're not just avoiding sin, but actively choosing what is good and allowing God to work in us through these choices. This obedience to conscience helps us to become more aware of God's presence and guidance, more willing to do what is right, and more open to receiving His grace. Our conscience becomes a reliable guide that helps us to navigate the complexities of life and to grow closer to God.

Prayer for Conscience

'Lord Jesus, help us to listen to and follow the voice of our conscience, recognizing it as Your voice speaking to us in the depths of our hearts. Give us the grace to form our conscience properly and to have the courage to act on what it tells us. May our obedience to conscience lead us to greater holiness and union with You. In Your name, Amen.'
