Parents Teaching Children About the Saints – Hebrews 12:1

2025-01-27

Introduction: A Great Cloud of Witnesses

Parents teaching children about the saints represents one of the most beautiful and important aspects of Catholic parenting. The author of Hebrews' description in Hebrews 12:1, "Therefore, since we are surrounded by so great a cloud of witnesses, let us also lay aside every weight, and sin which clings so closely, and let us run with endurance the race that is set before us," provides the foundation for understanding how to teach children about the saints and help them find role models in the faith. This teaching involves recognizing that the saints are not just historical figures but living examples of what it means to follow Christ and that they can serve as powerful role models for our children.

When we understand parents teaching children about the saints, we recognize that this is not just about teaching history but about introducing children to real people who lived holy lives and can inspire them to do the same. This understanding helps us to see that teaching about the saints is about helping children find role models who can guide them in their own journey of faith.

Understanding the Biblical Foundation of the Saints

Parents teaching children about the saints begins with understanding the biblical foundation of the saints and their importance in the life of faith. The Bible repeatedly emphasizes the importance of learning from the examples of faithful people, from the Old Testament's examples of holy men and women to the New Testament's teachings about the communion of saints. Scripture consistently shows that we can learn from the examples of those who have gone before us in faith.

This biblical foundation shows that parents teaching children about the saints is about recognizing that this is God's plan for helping children grow in faith. When we understand this, we can approach this teaching with reverence and enthusiasm.

Introducing Children to the Saints

Parents teaching children about the saints includes introducing children to the saints in ways that are appropriate for their age and understanding. This involves telling stories about the saints that children can relate to, finding saints who are particularly relevant to children's interests and experiences, using books and other resources to make the saints come alive, and finding ways to help children see the saints as real people. The key is to make the saints accessible and inspiring for children.

This introduction shows that parents teaching children about the saints is about making the saints real and relevant to children's lives. When we introduce children to the saints effectively, we help them find role models who can inspire them to live holy lives.

Helping Children Relate to the Saints

Parents teaching children about the saints involves helping children relate to the saints and see them as role models for their own lives. This might involve helping children understand how the saints faced challenges similar to those children face today, showing children how the saints practiced virtues that children can also practice, or finding ways to help children see how they can follow the saints' examples in their own lives. The key is to help children see the saints as relevant and inspiring role models.

This relationship building shows that parents teaching children about the saints is about helping children see the saints as real people they can learn from and imitate. When we help children relate to the saints, we help them find inspiration and guidance for their own journey of faith.

Conclusion: Inspiring Role Models

Parents teaching children about the saints reveals that the saints are powerful role models who can inspire children to live holy lives. When we teach our children about the saints, we help them find inspiration and guidance for their own journey of faith.

As we work to teach our children about the saints, let us remember that we are surrounded by a great cloud of witnesses. When we teach our children about the saints with love and enthusiasm, we help them find role models who can inspire them to run with endurance the race that is set before them.
