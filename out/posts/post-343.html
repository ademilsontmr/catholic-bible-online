The Solemnity of Corpus Christi

2025-02-02

Introduction: Corpus Christi

The Eucharist is the heart of the Church. We profess, celebrate, receive, and adore the Body and Blood of Christ.

Scriptural Roots

John 6, the institution narratives (Mt 26; Mk 14; Lk 22; 1 Cor 11) manifest the total gift of Christ.

Dimensions of the Mystery

  • Sacrifice: The Mass makes present the one sacrifice of the Cross.
  • Memorial: Not mere recollection, but sacramental presence.
  • Communion: Union with Christ and with the Church.
  • Adoration: We adore Christ substantially present under the species.

From Altar to Street

Processions express public faith and mission: charity to the poor flows from Eucharistic adoration.

Prayer

Jesus in the Eucharist, make us living tabernacles of Your love. Amen.
