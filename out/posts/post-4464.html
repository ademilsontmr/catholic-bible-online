Day 11: The Armor of God (Ephesians 6:10-18)

Day 11 of your 40-day journey with Saint Michael focuses on the spiritual armor described in Ephesians 6:10-18 and how to put it on for effective spiritual warfare. This day helps you understand the practical tools that God has provided for your spiritual protection and how to use them effectively. Understanding the armor of God helps you recognize that you are not defenseless in spiritual warfare, but have been given powerful spiritual weapons and protection. Day 11 demonstrates that spiritual warfare requires preparation and equipment, and that God has provided everything you need to stand firm against spiritual attacks.

The Call to Put on the Armor

Ephesians 6:10 begins with a call to 'be strong in the Lord and in the strength of his might.' This call emphasizes that spiritual strength comes from God, not from our own power. The passage then instructs us to 'put on the whole armor of God' so that we can stand against the schemes of the devil. This instruction is not optional but essential for effective spiritual warfare.

Understanding this call helps you recognize the importance of being properly equipped for spiritual battle.

The Belt of Truth

The belt of truth is the first piece of spiritual armor mentioned. In ancient warfare, the belt held all the other pieces of armor together and provided support for the soldier's weapons. In spiritual warfare, truth serves as the foundation that holds everything else together. Wearing the belt of truth means living in accordance with God's truth and rejecting lies and deception.

Understanding the belt of truth helps you recognize the importance of truth in spiritual warfare.

The Breastplate of Righteousness

The breastplate of righteousness protects the heart and vital organs. In spiritual warfare, righteousness protects your spiritual heart and keeps you in right relationship with God. This righteousness comes from Christ and is maintained through living a holy life and seeking God's forgiveness when you fall short.

Understanding the breastplate of righteousness helps you appreciate the importance of holiness in spiritual protection.

The Shoes of the Gospel of Peace

The shoes of the gospel of peace enable you to stand firm and to move forward in spreading the good news. In spiritual warfare, these shoes give you stability and enable you to share the peace of Christ with others. They also help you maintain inner peace even in the midst of spiritual battles.

Understanding the shoes of the gospel of peace helps you recognize the importance of peace in spiritual warfare.

The Shield of Faith

The shield of faith is described as being able to 'quench all the flaming darts of the evil one.' In spiritual warfare, faith serves as your primary defense against spiritual attacks. This faith is not just intellectual belief, but active trust in God and His promises. The shield of faith protects you from doubt, fear, and other spiritual attacks.

Understanding the shield of faith helps you appreciate the power of faith in spiritual protection.

The Helmet of Salvation

The helmet of salvation protects your mind and thoughts. In spiritual warfare, the helmet of salvation protects you from attacks on your mind, such as doubt, confusion, and despair. This helmet reminds you of your salvation and gives you confidence in your relationship with God.

Understanding the helmet of salvation helps you recognize the importance of protecting your mind in spiritual warfare.

The Sword of the Spirit

The sword of the Spirit is the word of God, which is both a defensive and offensive weapon in spiritual warfare. This sword enables you to defend yourself against spiritual attacks and to counter the lies of the enemy with God's truth. The sword of the Spirit is your primary offensive weapon in spiritual warfare.

Understanding the sword of the Spirit helps you appreciate the power of God's word in spiritual battle.

Prayer as the Foundation

Ephesians 6:18 emphasizes the importance of prayer as the foundation of spiritual warfare. Prayer is not just another piece of armor, but the means by which you access all the other pieces of armor and maintain your connection with God. Prayer enables you to stay alert and to persevere in spiritual warfare.

Understanding the role of prayer helps you recognize its central importance in spiritual warfare.

Putting on the Armor Daily

Putting on the armor of God is not a one-time event, but a daily practice. Each day, you need to consciously put on each piece of armor through prayer and spiritual practices. This daily practice helps you maintain spiritual protection and effectiveness in spiritual warfare.

Understanding the need for daily practice helps you develop a consistent spiritual discipline.

Conclusion: Fully Equipped

The armor of God provides you with complete spiritual protection and the tools needed for effective spiritual warfare. Understanding and using this armor helps you stand firm against spiritual attacks and to engage effectively in spiritual warfare. This understanding gives you confidence and strength as you face spiritual challenges, knowing that you are fully equipped for battle.
