The Feast of the Sacred Heart of Jesus

2025-02-02

Introduction: Sacred Heart

From the pierced side (Jn 19:34) flows the Church. The Heart of Jesus reveals divine-human love.

Devotional Path

  • First Fridays and Holy Hour of reparation.
  • Consecration to the Sacred Heart at home and parish.
  • Practice of mercy and meekness in daily life.

Prayer

Most Sacred Heart of Jesus, have mercy on us. Make our hearts like unto Yours. Amen.
