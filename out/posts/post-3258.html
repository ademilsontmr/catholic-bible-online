The Emmaus encounter is not just about a historical event but about how Jesus continues to walk with us and reveal Himself to us. The Emmaus encounter helps believers understand Jesus' presence in their lives.

The Emmaus encounter is recorded in the Gospel of Luke, chapter 24, showing its importance in revealing how Jesus appears to His disciples after His resurrection. This biblical foundation shows that the Emmaus encounter is not just a Church tradition but has deep roots in God's revelation. The Emmaus encounter helps believers understand the biblical model of discipleship.
