Day 2: Trusting in God's Heavenly Hosts

Day 2 of your 40-day journey with Saint Michael focuses on developing trust in God's heavenly hosts - the vast army of angels that serves God and protects His people. Understanding the reality of these heavenly hosts helps you appreciate the comprehensive spiritual protection that surrounds you and the various ways in which these powerful spiritual beings work together for your good. This day helps you develop confidence in the entire angelic realm and to recognize that you have multiple layers of spiritual protection available to you. Day 2 demonstrates that you are never alone and that powerful spiritual allies are constantly working on your behalf.

The Reality of God's Heavenly Hosts

God's heavenly hosts are not a metaphor or a figure of speech, but a real and powerful spiritual reality. The Bible and Church tradition teach that there are countless angels serving God, organized in a hierarchy of different ranks and responsibilities. These heavenly hosts are not distant or indifferent, but actively involved in protecting and serving God's people.

Understanding the reality of these heavenly hosts helps you appreciate the scope of spiritual protection available to you.

The Hierarchy of the Heavenly Hosts

The heavenly hosts are organized in a hierarchy of nine choirs: Seraphim, Cherubim, Thrones, Dominions, Virtues, Powers, Principalities, Archangels, and Angels. Each choir has specific roles and responsibilities in serving God and protecting His people. Saint Michael, as Prince of the Heavenly Hosts, coordinates and directs the work of all these angels.

Understanding this hierarchy helps you appreciate the organization and efficiency of God's spiritual protection system.

How the Heavenly Hosts Protect You

The heavenly hosts protect you in multiple ways. Some angels are assigned to specific areas of protection, such as your guardian angel who is with you constantly. Others work together to create a spiritual shield around you, your family, and your community. The heavenly hosts also work to prevent spiritual attacks and to strengthen you in times of spiritual difficulty.

Understanding how the heavenly hosts protect you helps you trust that you are well-guarded against spiritual dangers.

Trusting in the Heavenly Hosts

Trusting in the heavenly hosts involves recognizing their reality, appreciating their power, and cooperating with their protection. This trust is not blind faith, but based on the knowledge that these angels are God's servants, working under His authority and for your good. Trusting in the heavenly hosts means believing that they are actively working to protect you and to help you grow in holiness.

This trust gives you confidence and peace, knowing that you are surrounded by powerful spiritual allies.

The Power of Collective Angelic Protection

The heavenly hosts work together as a unified force, creating a powerful system of spiritual protection. When you invoke Saint Michael's help, you're not just calling upon one angel, but accessing the power and protection of the entire angelic realm. This collective protection is far more powerful than individual angelic assistance and provides comprehensive coverage against spiritual threats.

Understanding the power of collective angelic protection helps you appreciate the strength of your spiritual defenses.

Cooperating with the Heavenly Hosts

Cooperating with the heavenly hosts involves living a life that welcomes their protection and assistance. This includes avoiding sin, which can weaken spiritual protection, and living in a way that honors God and invites angelic assistance. It also involves being aware of the spiritual realm and being open to the guidance and protection that the angels provide.

Cooperating with the heavenly hosts strengthens your relationship with them and makes their protection more effective.

Recognizing Angelic Presence

While you may not always be aware of the heavenly hosts' presence, they are constantly working around you. You can become more aware of their presence through prayer, spiritual sensitivity, and by paying attention to the ways in which you are protected and guided. Sometimes you may experience their presence through feelings of peace, protection, or guidance.

Recognizing angelic presence helps you appreciate the reality of their protection and strengthens your trust in their assistance.

Conclusion: A Trustworthy Protection

God's heavenly hosts are a trustworthy and powerful source of spiritual protection. Understanding their reality, hierarchy, and methods of protection helps you develop confidence in their assistance and to trust that you are well-protected against spiritual dangers. This trust gives you peace and confidence as you continue your spiritual journey with Saint Michael.
