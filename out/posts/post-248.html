Early Saints and Joseph

The early saints' recognition of Joseph was based primarily on the Gospel accounts of his life and his role in the Holy Family. They recognized Joseph's righteousness, his obedience to God's will, and his protective care for Mary and Jesus as important virtues to imitate.
