Holiness Through Praying the Liturgy of the Hours

2024-12-20

Introduction: The Prayer of the Church

Holiness through praying the Liturgy of the Hours is about recognizing that this ancient form of prayer is not just a devotion for priests and religious, but a powerful means of sanctification that is available to all Christians. The Liturgy of the Hours, also known as the Divine Office, is the official prayer of the Church that sanctifies the different hours of the day and unites us with the prayer of the entire Church throughout the world. When we pray the Liturgy of the Hours, we're not just saying prayers, but participating in the Church's continuous worship of God and allowing Him to transform us through this prayer.

Many people think that the Liturgy of the Hours is too complicated or too time-consuming for lay people, or that it's only for those who are very advanced in the spiritual life. However, the Church encourages all Christians to pray at least some part of the Liturgy of the Hours, and there are many resources available to help people get started. The Liturgy of the Hours is not meant to replace personal prayer, but to complement it and to help us develop a rhythm of prayer that sanctifies our entire day.

How to Pray the Liturgy of the Hours

Praying the Liturgy of the Hours involves several important practices. First, it means choosing a time or times during the day to pray, such as morning, evening, or both. Second, it means using the appropriate prayers for the time of day, which include psalms, readings from Scripture, and intercessions. Third, it means praying with attention and devotion, not just going through the motions, but truly entering into the prayer of the Church. Fourth, it means being consistent and persevering, even when it's difficult or when we don't feel like praying. Fifth, it means allowing the prayers to shape our thoughts and attitudes throughout the day.

Conclusion: Prayer as Sanctification

Praying the Liturgy of the Hours leads to holiness because it helps us to develop a rhythm of prayer that sanctifies our entire day and unites us with the prayer of the Church. When we pray the Liturgy of the Hours, we're not just fulfilling a religious obligation, but allowing God to work in us through His Word and through the prayers of the Church. This prayer transforms us from the inside out, making us more aware of God's presence, more open to His guidance, and more willing to allow Him to work in our lives.

Prayer for the Liturgy of the Hours

'Lord Jesus, help us to pray the Liturgy of the Hours with devotion and love, recognizing it as the prayer of Your Church and a means of sanctifying our day. Give us the grace to be consistent in this prayer and to allow it to transform our hearts and minds. May our participation in the Liturgy of the Hours lead us to greater holiness and union with You. In Your name, Amen.'
