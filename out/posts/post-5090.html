The Biblical Foundation: Tell the Next Generation

The Bible teaches us about the importance of telling stories to the next generation. Psalm 78:4 instructs us to 'tell the next generation the praiseworthy deeds of the Lord, his power, and the wonders he has done.' Additionally, Deuteronomy 6:6-7 tells us to 'Impress these commands on your children. Talk about them when you sit at home.'
