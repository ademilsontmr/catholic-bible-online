The Biblical Foundation: Tell the Next Generation

The Bible teaches us about the importance of sharing the stories of God's works. Psalm 78:4 instructs us to 'tell the next generation the praiseworthy deeds of the Lord.' Additionally, Deuteronomy 6:6-7 tells us to 'Impress these commands on your children. Talk about them when you sit at home and when you walk along the road, when you lie down and when you get up.' Understanding this biblical foundation helps you recognize that sharing the stories of Jesus and the Apostles is important for faith formation.
