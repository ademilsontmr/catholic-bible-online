The Feast of the Baptism of the Lord

2025-02-02

Introduction: The Baptism of the Lord

Jesus stands among sinners at the Jordan, not to be purified but to sanctify the waters for us. The heavens open; the Spirit descends; the Father declares, “You are my beloved Son.”

Why It Matters

Christ’s baptism reveals solidarity and mission. He foreshadows the Paschal Mystery: descent (death) and rising (new life). He makes the waters a gateway into divine life.

Trinitarian Epiphany

The Father’s voice, the Son’s obedience, and the Spirit’s anointing reveal God as communion. Our baptism makes us beloved sons and daughters in the Son.

Living Our Baptism

  • Priestly: offer your daily work as spiritual worship.
  • Prophetic: speak truth in charity.
  • Royal: govern your passions and defend the vulnerable.

Practices

Renew your baptismal promises; mark your baptism date; use holy water; seek reconciliation often; hear the Father’s voice over every other.

Prayer

Father of mercies, renew in us the grace of our baptism. Make us faithful witnesses of your belovedness in the world. Amen.
