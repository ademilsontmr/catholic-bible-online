Introduction: The Source of True Wisdom

Finding wisdom in Christ during school challenges begins with understanding that true wisdom comes from God. Proverbs 2:6 tells us, "For the Lord gives wisdom; from his mouth come knowledge and understanding." When we understand finding wisdom in Christ during school challenges, we recognize that God is the source of all wisdom and that He wants to help us navigate the difficulties we face in our academic journey.
