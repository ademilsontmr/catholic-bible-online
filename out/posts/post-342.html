Pentecost Sunday

2025-02-02

Introduction: Pentecost

Fifty days after Easter, the Spirit descends upon the disciples gathered in prayer with Mary. The fearful become bold witnesses.

Scripture and Promise

Acts 2 fulfills Joel 3 and Jesus' promise (Jn 14–16). The Spirit gives kerygma power, unity beyond languages, and interior renewal.

Gifts and Fruits

  • Gifts: wisdom, understanding, counsel, fortitude, knowledge, piety, fear of the Lord.
  • Fruits: charity, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, self‑control.

Spiritual Life

Docility to the Spirit in prayer, lectio divina, sacraments, and mission. He builds communion and sends us to the peripheries.

Prayer

Come, Holy Spirit, fill the hearts of Your faithful and enkindle in them the fire of Your love. Amen.
