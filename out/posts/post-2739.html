Catholic Politics: A Complete Guide to Citizen Participation and Civic Engagement

2025-01-15

What is Catholic Political Engagement?

Catholic political engagement is the active participation of Catholics in political life, civic affairs, and public policy, guided by Catholic social teaching and moral principles. This approach emphasizes the importance of citizen participation in democratic processes, working for the common good, and advocating for policies that promote human dignity, justice, and solidarity. Catholic political engagement recognizes that politics is a noble vocation when pursued with integrity and service to others.

The Foundation of Catholic Political Ethics

Catholic political engagement is built on several key principles:

  • Human dignity and the common good
  • Solidarity and subsidiarity
  • Justice and peace
  • Truth and integrity
  • Service and leadership

Key Principles

Catholic social teaching provides essential guidance:
