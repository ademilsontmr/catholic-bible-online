The title 'Christ' means 'the anointed one' and reveals Jesus as the Messiah promised in the Old Testament. Catholic tradition sees this title as essential for understanding that Jesus is the fulfillment of God's promises and the long-awaited Messiah. The title Christ shows us that Jesus is the one chosen and anointed by God to save His people. This title shows that Jesus' titles are not just about His nature but about His role in salvation history. Christ - the anointed one helps believers understand Jesus' role as the promised Messiah.
