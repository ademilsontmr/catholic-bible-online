Catholic Saints Who Intercede for World Peace

The Catholic Church teaches that saints in heaven can intercede for us before God, and many saints are particularly known for their intercession for peace. These holy men and women can be powerful allies in our prayers for world peace. Understanding Catholic saints who intercede for world peace helps you appreciate the Church's spiritual resources for peace work and provides guidance for seeking their intercession. These saints demonstrate that we are not alone in our efforts for peace, but are supported by a great cloud of witnesses who have gone before us.

Saint Michael the Archangel

Saint Michael the Archangel is known as the defender of the Church and a powerful intercessor for peace. His role in spiritual warfare makes him particularly effective for prayers against the forces of evil that threaten peace. Understanding Saint Michael's intercession helps you appreciate his power for peace work.

Saint Francis of Assisi

Saint Francis of Assisi, known as the patron saint of peace, is a powerful intercessor for peace. His life of peacemaking and his famous prayer for peace make him particularly effective for peace intentions. Understanding Saint Francis's intercession helps you appreciate his special role in peace work.

Saint Therese of Lisieux

Saint Therese of Lisieux, known as the Little Flower, intercedes for peace through her 'little way' of love and sacrifice. Her spiritual approach to peacemaking makes her effective for prayers for peace in daily life. Understanding Saint Therese's intercession helps you appreciate the power of small acts for peace.

Saint John Paul II

Saint John Paul II, who worked tirelessly for peace during his papacy, is a powerful intercessor for peace. His experience in peace work and his deep commitment to peace make him particularly effective for peace intentions. Understanding Saint John Paul II's intercession helps you appreciate his leadership in peace work.

Saint Mother Teresa

Saint Mother Teresa, who worked for peace through service to the poor, intercedes for peace through her ministry of love and compassion. Her work for peace through service makes her effective for prayers for peace through acts of mercy. Understanding Saint Mother Teresa's intercession helps you appreciate the connection between service and peace.

Saint Maximilian Kolbe, who gave his life for peace, intercedes for peace through his ultimate sacrifice. Understanding Saint Maximilian Kolbe's intercession helps you appreciate the power of sacrifice for peace.

Understanding Saint Joan of Arc's intercession helps you appreciate the courage needed for peace work.

His work for peace through reconciliation makes him effective for prayers for peace in divided communities. Understanding Saint Anthony's intercession helps you appreciate the importance of reconciliation for peace.
