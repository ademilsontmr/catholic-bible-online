Prayer for Families as a Ministry

Praying for families is an important ministry that offers spiritual support and guidance to the domestic church. Catholic tradition sees prayer for families as essential for understanding that families are the foundation of society and the Church and need our spiritual support. Prayer for families is not just about asking for blessings but about supporting families in their mission to grow in holiness. This understanding shows that praying for families is not just a nice gesture but an important ministry. Praying for families helps support the domestic church and build up society.

Different Ways to Pray

Different ways to pray help provide appropriate spiritual support for families.
