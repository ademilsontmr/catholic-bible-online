Introduction: The Ascension of the Lord

The Ascension crowns the Paschal Mystery. Jesus, true God and true Man, returns to the Father and opens for us the way to heaven. Far from an absence, the Ascension inaugurates a new presence: sacramental and ecclesial.

Biblical Foundation

Acts 1:6-11 narrates Jesus' departure and the angelic promise of His return. Luke 24:50-53 highlights the blessing; Ephesians 1:20-23 proclaims Christ's lordship over all things.

Theological Meaning

  • Exaltation: Christ sits at the right hand of the Father, interceding for us.
  • Mission: The Church is sent to evangelize all peoples.
  • Hope: Where the Head has gone, the Body hopes to follow.

Living the Mystery

Contemplate Christ in glory; embrace the mission; cultivate heavenly mindedness while serving on earth.

Prayer

Lord Jesus, who ascended in glory, inflame our hearts with missionary zeal and hope of heaven. Amen.
