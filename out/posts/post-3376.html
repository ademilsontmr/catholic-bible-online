The Biblical Foundation

Lighting candles has its roots in Scripture, particularly in the Old Testament practice of lighting lamps in the temple and in the New Testament references to Christ as the Light of the World. Catholic tradition sees this biblical foundation as essential for understanding the historical and theological significance of lighting candles.
