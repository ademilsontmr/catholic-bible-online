The Biblical Foundation: Your Kingdom Come

The Bible teaches us about heaven and God's kingdom. Matthew 6:10 records Jesus teaching us to pray, 'Your kingdom come, your will be done, on earth as it is in heaven.' Additionally, Revelation 21:4 promises that in heaven 'He will wipe every tear from their eyes. There will be no more death or mourning or crying or pain.' Understanding this biblical foundation helps you recognize that heaven and God's kingdom are important to our faith.
