The Feast of the Transfiguration

2025-02-02

Introduction: The Transfiguration

Before the Passion, Jesus shines with divine light (Mt 17; Mk 9; Lk 9). Moses and Elijah testify: the Law and the Prophets converge in Him.

Meaning for Discipleship

Tabor prepares us for Calvary. Prayer changes our gaze; the Cross becomes luminous.

Liturgical Resonance

The light of Christ bathes the Church in Baptism and the Eucharist; we are called to be radiant witnesses.

Prayer

Lord, transfigure us with Your grace, that we may reflect Your glory. Amen.
