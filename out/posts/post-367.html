Biblical Foundation of Detachment

The Bible provides numerous examples of how fasting and detachment work together. Fasting and the First Beatitude

Fasting is deeply connected to the first beatitude: "Blessed are the poor in spirit, for theirs is the kingdom of heaven." When we fast, we practice poverty of spirit by recognizing our dependence on God rather than material things. This practice helps us develop the spiritual poverty that Jesus calls us to.
