How Novenas Foster Intimacy with Jesus and Mary

2024-12-19

Introduction: Growing in Intimacy

Intimacy with Jesus and Mary is not just about knowing about them, but about knowing them personally and loving them deeply. Novenas provide a powerful way to foster this intimacy, helping us to develop a more personal and loving relationship with our Savior and His Mother. Through nine days of focused prayer, we can grow closer to Jesus and Mary and experience their love and care for us in a deeper way.

When we pray novenas to Jesus and Mary, we are not just going through religious motions, but entering into a personal relationship with them. We learn to speak to them as we would to dear friends, sharing our joys, sorrows, hopes, and fears. This personal relationship transforms our prayer from a formal ritual into a loving conversation that deepens our intimacy with them.

How Novenas Deepen Intimacy

Novenas deepen intimacy with Jesus and Mary in several ways. First, they provide regular opportunities for us to spend time with them and to share our hearts with them. Second, they help us to develop the habit of turning to them in all circumstances, making them a constant presence in our lives.

Third, they help us to learn more about their lives and teachings, which helps us to understand them better and to love them more deeply. Fourth, they help us to experience their intercession and care for us, which strengthens our trust in them. Finally, they help us to develop a greater desire to imitate their example and to grow in holiness.

The Fruits of Intimate Relationship

Developing intimacy with Jesus and Mary through novenas brings many fruits. We experience greater peace and joy as we grow closer to them and experience their love for us. We become more confident in their care and more willing to turn to them in times of need. We develop a greater desire to serve them and to spread their love to others.

This intimacy also helps us to grow in holiness, as we learn to imitate their example and to follow their guidance. We become more aware of their presence in our lives and more responsive to their invitations to grow closer to them. This relationship becomes a source of great strength and encouragement in our spiritual journey.

Conclusion: A Loving Relationship

Novenas as a way of fostering intimacy with Jesus and Mary are a gift that can transform our spiritual life and bring us closer to heaven. This intimacy is not about emotional feelings, but about a real and personal relationship with our Savior and His Mother. When we develop this intimacy through novenas, we open ourselves to the love and care that Jesus and Mary want to give us.

This relationship becomes a source of great joy and a foundation for spiritual growth. It helps us to experience the love of God in a more personal way and to grow in our desire to serve Him. Over time, this intimacy becomes the most important relationship in our lives and a source of great spiritual strength.

Prayer for Intimacy with Jesus and Mary

'Lord Jesus and Blessed Virgin Mary, help us to grow in intimacy with You through our novenas. Draw us closer to Your hearts, help us to love You more deeply, and guide us in our spiritual journey. In Your name, Amen.'
