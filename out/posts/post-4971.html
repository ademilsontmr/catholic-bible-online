The Biblical Foundation: Train Up a Child

The Bible teaches us about the importance of teaching children about morality and faith. Proverbs 22:6 instructs us to 'Train up a child in the way he should go, and when he is old he will not depart from it.' Additionally, Deuteronomy 6:6-7 tells us to 'Impress these commands on your children. Talk about them when you sit at home and when you walk along the road, when you lie down and when you get up.' Understanding this biblical foundation helps you recognize that teaching about morality and faith is a continuous process.
