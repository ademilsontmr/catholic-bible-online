Clare as a Model of Poverty and Prayer

Saint Clare of Assisi was a remarkable woman who founded the Poor Clares and lived a life of radical poverty and prayer. Catholic tradition sees Clare as essential for understanding that women can be powerful spiritual leaders and that poverty and prayer are paths to holiness. Clare was not just a follower of Saint Francis but a spiritual leader in her own right. This understanding shows that Clare is not just a historical figure but a relevant example for contemporary spiritual life. Saint Clare helps believers understand the importance of poverty and prayer.
