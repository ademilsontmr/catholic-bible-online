Day 32: Saint Michael as Guardian of the Eucharist

Day 32 of your 40-day journey with Saint Michael focuses on understanding his special role as guardian of the Eucharist and protector of this sacred mystery. The Eucharist as the Most Sacred Gift

The Eucharist is the most sacred gift that Christ has given to His Church, containing His real presence and being the source and summit of the Christian life. As the most precious gift, the Eucharist requires special protection and reverence. Understanding the Eucharist as the most sacred gift helps you appreciate why Saint Michael has been entrusted with its protection and why this role is so important.

This understanding helps you appreciate the importance of Saint Michael's role as guardian of the Eucharist.
