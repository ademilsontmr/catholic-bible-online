The Feast of the Immaculate Heart of Mary

2025-02-02

Introduction: Immaculate Heart of Mary

Mary keeps and ponders the mysteries of Christ (Lk 2:19,51). Her Heart mirrors the Gospel.

Biblical Glimpses

  • Annunciation: fiat of love.
  • Visitation: charity in action.
  • At the Cross: maternal fidelity.

Christian Life

Imitation of Mary: prayer, purity, service, and consecration to her Heart for a deeper union with Jesus.

Prayer

Immaculate Heart of Mary, form us into true disciples of your Son. Amen.
